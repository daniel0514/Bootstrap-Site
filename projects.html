Bread Calculator

Tired of using a spread sheet to calculate the amount of bread needed for your heroes to reach a certain level? Why not just let Bread Calculator do the work for you? Base on the breads you currently have, Bread Calculator will tell you the optimal amount of bread needed for your heroes!

Bread Calculator is an Android app, written in Android Studio, to calculate the optimal amount of breads required to achieve certain level for heroes in the mobile game "Crusaders Quest". Each bread provides certain amount of experience for heroes in the game to advance in level. There are costs consuming breads. The app uses Lowest Cost First Search to find the combination of bread that result in lowest cost and lowest experience from the breads enough for heroes to reach a certain level. Consumption button was implemented for convenience of the users. The amount of bread will be removed from the inventory once the list is consumed.

Ever had the situation when you arrive at the restaurant but unable to find a parking spot nearby? You are in luck! ParkNEat will find nearby parking spots for you (however we do not guarantee the spot is empty). ParkNEat is a web application that locates restaurants near you (or a location of your choice) and display the parking spots near the restaurants of your choice. ParkNEat also allows you to see comments and rating from other users. So what are you waiting for? Start using ParkNEat

ParkNEat is developed in Java (with Eclipse IDE) and Google Web Toolkit. The web application uses Yelp's API to retrieve locations of restaurants, and parking locations are obtained from datasets from City of Vancouver Open Data Catalogue. These data are then stored onto Google's NoSQL database for persistence. When needed, data is retrieved from the database and being displayed. Other types of data include user generated restaurant rating and reviews (not from Yelp). Twitter API is included for users to twit about the experience with ParkNEat.

Shop Online!

Shop Online! is a website made for online shopping experience (for custoemrs) and inventory management (for managers and clerks). Customers can added items (if available) to their baskets and checkout items at once. Managers or clerks can create reports of top selling items, daily sells, and inventory counts. The website also has a login system to determine user information once accounts are created.

Shop Online is written in PHP and HTML, and the website uses MySQL database for data storage. SQL queries are specifically formulated based on user inputs and reflects exactly the behaviour we expect on the SQL server. The results of queries are then displayed in a readable format without any modification to the results For security, the website uses POST method in form handling to hide information from users. Passwords are encrypted with Blowfish Encryption and then stored in the database.
